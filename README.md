The hospital is planning to introduce an AI-based medical diagnosis system that can analyze patient symptoms, medical history, and lab reports.

Use Case:
Compare traditional diagnosis with the AI-assisted diagnosis system in terms of speed, accuracy, and patient care. Explain how the AI system helps doctors make faster decisions, reduces human errors, and improves treatment quality for patients.

Comparative Analysis Prompt:
“Act as a healthcare analyst. Compare the traditional medical diagnosis process with an AI-assisted medical diagnosis system in a hospital environment. Analyze both approaches based on speed of diagnosis, accuracy in identifying diseases, ability to handle large numbers of patients, and overall quality of patient care. Explain how AI can support doctors by analyzing patient symptoms, medical history, and lab reports, helping them make faster and more informed decisions. Also discuss possible limitations or challenges of using AI in healthcare.”

2.Agriculture Field
Scenario:
A farmer wants to increase crop yield but faces problems like unpredictable weather, pest attacks, and inefficient irrigation. The farmer is considering using AI-based smart farming technology that monitors soil conditions and weather patterns.
Use Case:
Compare traditional farming methods with AI-based smart farming in terms of crop monitoring, water usage, and productivity. Explain how AI helps farmers make better decisions about irrigation, fertilizer use, and pest control.

Comparative Analysis Prompts (Agriculture Field):
“Compare and contrast traditional farming methods with AI-based smart farming technologies.”
“How does AI-based crop monitoring differ from manual crop monitoring in agriculture?”

Experiential Perspective Prompts (Agriculture Field):
“As a farmer, what benefits do you experience when using AI-based smart farming technology to monitor crops and soil conditions?”
“From a farmer’s perspective, describe the challenges of managing crops using traditional farming methods.”
“As a farmer using AI-powered smart farming tools, how do they help you make better decisions about irrigation, fertilizers, and pest control?”


Everyday Functioning Prompts (Agriculture Field):
“How is AI-based smart farming technology used in the daily activities of farmers to monitor soil conditions, weather patterns, and crop health?”
“Describe how AI helps farmers in everyday farming tasks such as irrigation management, fertilizer application, and pest control.”
“How can AI tools support farmers in their daily crop monitoring and decision-making processes?”
